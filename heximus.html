HEXIMUS

(2019)

Play Now >>

Platform :
Windows, Mac, & Linux

Team Size :
1

Affiliation :
Fitchburg State University

Development Roles :
Programmer / Artist / Sound Designer / Composer

Description :

A casual local multiplayer game to be played with friends. Plant bombs anywhere in your territory to expand your borders. Any tiles you convert have a chance to drop upgrades you can use to improve your combat prowess. Can you take out your opponent before succumbing to one of their bombs?

Project Reflection :

This was the first digital game I polished to a presentable state. At this point, much of the development process for games was fairly new to me, and many of the technologies and tools presented me with a challenging learning curve. The creation of UI elements, sound effects, 3-D models, and music, along with the programming of the game gave me a sense for the different roles usually delegated in group projects. I put quite a bit more time into the aesthetics and sound design than programming, with those areas being new to me.
