Project Reflection :

This project presented me with some very unique circumstantial challenges. This was developed around the time covid started to gain traction, and as such the development process was heavily strained, shifting settings from in person to online. In spite of this, our group managed to keep to the self determined deadlines and produced a well polished product. I was challenged with developing a dynamic node-based pathing system for enemies, tools for use by level designers, and creating dynamic aiming scripts which accounted for the various types of towers.
