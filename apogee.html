Project Reflection :

This project presented some very unique challenges which were new to me at the time. This was developed roughly around the time covid started to gain traction, and as such, the development process was impacted fairly severely. Our team coordination transitioned from one of in person meetings to solely online interactions. In spite of this, our group managed to keep to the self determined deadlines and produced a fairly well polished product. I was challenged with developing a dynamic node-based pathing system for enemies, as well as creating dynamic projectile aiming scripts which accounted for the various types of towers.
